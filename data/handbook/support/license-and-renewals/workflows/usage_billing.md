## Usage billing support workflow

Refer to this page when a user has questions or issues related to **usage-based pricing and billing for GitLab Duo Agent Platform (DAP)** or **GitLab Credits (including Free .com namespaces)**. The **source of truth** for Support is the internal **Usage Billing – Support Guide** Google Doc.

> **Note:** This page is an entry point for Support. It intentionally routes you to the detailed guide rather than duplicating it.

### Duo Core changes in 18.11

As of **GitLab 18.11 (2026-04-16)**, **Duo Core** changes in two key ways:

- **Classic Chat is removed for Duo Core users**, only **Agentic Chat** remains, and it is **usage-billed**.  
- **Code Suggestions** (Code Completion and Code Generation) for Duo Core become **usage-billed at a flat rate of 50 requests per credit**, regardless of the underlying model.

These changes:

- **Do not affect Duo Pro or Duo Enterprise** seat holders (their classic features remain unlimited and unmetered).  
- **Do not change included promotional credits** (Premium: **12 credits/user/month**, Ultimate: **24 credits/user/month**).  
- **Apply even to customers on versions earlier than 18.11**, subject to the existing **18.6.6 minimum for Self-Managed DAP access**.

For a full breakdown (including impact by subscription, deployment type, and a detailed change-matrix table), please review the internal **Usage Billing – Support Guide** section below:
