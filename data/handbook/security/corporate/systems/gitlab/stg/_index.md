## Access Process

### Creating an Account

If no account exists, create an [access request](https://gitlab.com/gitlab-com/team-member-epics/access-requests/-/work_items/new?description_template=GitLabStaging_Request) and assign it to your manager.

### Signing In

Sign in at [staging.gitlab.com](https://staging.gitlab.com/users/sign_in) with your email and password. Only GitLab email addresses are allowed.

### Password Reset

If you already have an account but do not know the password, use the [password reset form](https://staging.gitlab.com/users/password/new). This sends a reset link to your GitLab email.
