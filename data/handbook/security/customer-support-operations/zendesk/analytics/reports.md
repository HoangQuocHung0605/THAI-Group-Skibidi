- All actions detailed on this page require at least light agent access to Zendesk analytics

{{% /alert %}}

## Seeing a list of reports

To see a list of reports, you would navigate to the [reports library page](https://gitlab.zendesk.com/explore/studio#/reports).

## Creating a report

{{% alert title="Note" color="info" %}}

- This requires write access to Zendesk analytics

{{% /alert %}}

To create a report:

1. Navigate to the [reports library page](https://gitlab.zendesk.com/explore/studio#/reports)
1. Click `New report` on the top-right  of the page
1. Select the dataset you wish to use
1. Click `Start report` at the bottom-right of the page
1. Fill out all information for the report (this will vary based off what you are making a report for)
1. Click `Save` at the top-right of the page

## Editing a report

{{% alert title="Note" color="info" %}}

- This requires either `Administrator` permissions or ownership of the report in question

{{% /alert %}}

To edit a report:

1. Navigate to the [reports library page](https://gitlab.zendesk.com/explore/studio#/reports)
1. Locate the report you wish to edit and click its name
1. Make the changes you wish to make
1. Click `Save` at the top-right of the page

## Cloning a report

{{% alert title="Note" color="info" %}}

- This requires write access to Zendesk analytics

{{% /alert %}}

To clone a report:

1. Navigate to the [reports library page](https://gitlab.zendesk.com/explore/studio#/reports)
1. Locate the report you wish to edit and click the three vertical dots to the right of it
1. Click `Clone`
1. Enter the new name to use for the clone
1. Select which dataset for the cloned report to use
1. Click `Clone` at the bottom-right of the popup

## Deleting a report

{{% alert title="Danger" color="danger" %}}

- This action is permanent and cannot be undone

{{% /alert %}}
{{% alert title="Note" color="info" %}}

- This requires either `Administrator` permissions or ownership of the report in question

{{% /alert %}}

To delete a report:

1. Navigate to the [reports library page](https://gitlab.zendesk.com/explore/studio#/reports)
1. Locate the report you wish to delete and click the three vertical dots to the right of it
1. Click `Delete`
1. Click `Yes, delete` to confirm the deletion
