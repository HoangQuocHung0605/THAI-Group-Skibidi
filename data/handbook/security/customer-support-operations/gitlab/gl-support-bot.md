## Understanding gl-support-bot

### What is gl-support-bot

For many of our scripts and automations, we use the [gl-support-bot](https://gitlab.com/gl-support-bot) user to act as a team wide service account. This is to ensure our automations are not tied to a specific human user and run consistently.
